Benefits for Customer

Meal orders are as simple as 1-2-3.

We advice or suggest Customers with the right Food Packages to ensure balanced nutrition based on their Worker's activities to enhance productivity. Making orders are simplified by recommending Food Packages and meals are automatically generated based on the specific Package specifications.

Once the meal Package has been selected we shortlist the Caterers and automate the Caterer tender process. Manual processes will be eliminated thus enhancing productivity.
